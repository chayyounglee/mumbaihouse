The Mumbai House
Indian Bistro



Enjoy classic Indian cuisine in the Beach with a view of Lake Ontario. Our specialty dishes include Seafood and Tandoori and our Butter Chicken is the best in Toronto. Come and try excellent fare at an affordable price. Check out our menu online. Executive Chef Mr. Hussain Ahmed specializes in Indian cuisine and has over 20 years experience.

The Mumbai House has been routinely hailed as the Top Indian Restaurant in Leslieville by media outlets including BlogTO and InSauga and has been featured in newspapers including The Toronto Star.

Whether it be an intimate experience for two, a large gathering, corporate event, catering for a special occasion or take-out, The Mumbai House is sure to please.
